PS . The Tax Deadline Changed For This Year

Because of a federal holiday, the deadline for filing taxes will be three days after the usual April 15. This is because April 15, 2016, falls on a Friday, the Emancipation Day, yet according to IRS policy, the tax filing deadline can’t be on a holiday or a weekend, meaning it will roll over to Monday, April 18, 2016.

“It was totally mind-boggling,” Aker said. “I was wondering, why are you here? I am home, I haven’t done anything ... Why are the marshals knocking on my door? It’s amazing.” Aker said he was arrested, shackled and taken to federal court. “I was told: ‘You owe $1,500.’ I just couldn’t believe it,” he told Fox 26. “I was taken before a judge surrounded by seven marshals.” Texas representative Gene Green, a Democrat, said it was unacceptable that US marshals are being used to collect decades old student loans. “There’s bound to be a better way to collect on a student loan debt that is so old,” he told the station. Aker is unlikely to be the only person to be surprised by marshals collecting on student loans.
